About Us

Literacy Lift is dedicated to empowering rural communities by enabling school students to share knowledge and essential skills with those who lack access to education. Our mission is to provide literacy, essential life skills, and education to rural areas where it’s needed the most.

Our Mission

We aim to create a world where education is accessible to all, bridging the literacy gap with the help of passionate school students.
